Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CivicConnect, reporting issues, and how we work to empower communities.

General Questions

What is CivicConnect?
CivicConnect is a platform designed to help citizens report civic issues in their locality, track progress, and stay engaged with community improvements.
How do I report an issue?
You can report an issue by visiting the "Report Issue" page, filling out the form with details such as locality, area, status, and description, and submitting it.
Is there a cost to use CivicConnect?
No, CivicConnect is completely free to use for all citizens. Our goal is to make civic engagement accessible to everyone.
Can I track the progress of my reported issue?
Yes, once you submit an issue, it will appear in the Issues Feed. You can track its progress as "Not Resolved," "Working," or "Resolved."
Do I need to create an account?
Currently, you can report issues without creating an account. However, future updates may include optional accounts for personalized tracking.
How does CivicConnect ensure issues are addressed?
We collaborate with local authorities and community volunteers to ensure reported issues are visible and prioritized for resolution.
Can I share issues on social media?
Yes, each issue card includes options to share on social platforms, helping raise awareness and encourage collective action.
What types of issues can I report?
You can report a wide range of civic problems such as potholes, garbage overflow, streetlight outages, drainage blockages, illegal parking, and more.
How can I contribute to resolving issues?
You can volunteer with local community groups, share issues on social media to raise awareness, or directly contact municipal authorities through CivicConnect.
Is my personal data safe?
Yes, CivicConnect values your privacy. We only collect the information necessary to report and track issues. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.
Can I edit or delete an issue I reported?
Currently, you cannot edit or delete submitted issues directly. However, you can contact our support team to request changes or removal.
Will CivicConnect expand to other cities?
Yes, our long-term vision is to expand CivicConnect to multiple cities across India, empowering citizens everywhere to take part in civic improvement.
How do I know if an issue is resolved?
Each issue card in the feed shows its progress status. Once marked "Resolved," it means the issue has been addressed by authorities or community volunteers.
Can businesses or organizations use CivicConnect?
Yes, businesses and organizations can use CivicConnect to report issues affecting their areas and collaborate with local authorities for solutions.
Does CivicConnect provide notifications?
Future updates will include optional notifications to alert you when the status of your reported issue changes.
How do I contact CivicConnect support?
You can reach us through the Contact page, where you’ll find a form and email address for support inquiries.
